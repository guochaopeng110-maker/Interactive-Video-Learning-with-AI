## What to build

Build the first complete student workspace slice from course entry through the student `KnowledgeGraph`, `Lesson` player, remediation, and progress.

This slice should demonstrate how a student consumes only published learning content and moves through a coherent learning path:

- student home or course entry
- course-level learning context
- read-only student `KnowledgeGraph`
- `Lesson` playback with branch-aware interaction framing
- remediation or review destination
- progress reflection and next-action continuity

The result should be a demoable student learning loop with clear separation from teacher draft and authoring states.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] A student can move from course entry into a read-only `KnowledgeGraph` and then into a published `Lesson`.
- [ ] The prototype shows a clear remediation or review path rather than leaving wrong-answer recovery implicit.
- [ ] Student-facing routes never expose teacher draft states or authoring actions.
